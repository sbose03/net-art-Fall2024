Airplane

The first plane that took flight was Kitty Hawk, invented by the Wright brothers in 1903.

Planes saw a rapid development thanks to war, in particular WW1 and WW2. The most dominant plane was the P-51 Mustang, built by North American Aviation, which would eventually become part of Boeing.

The most commonly built commercial plane is the Boeing 737, with over 11,000 planes built.
